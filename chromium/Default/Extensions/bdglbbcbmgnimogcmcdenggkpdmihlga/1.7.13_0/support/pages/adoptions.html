Ad Options: Why Did I Get This Ad?

To create a more customized online experience for consumers, some of the ads you may receive from The extension and its networks are tailored to previous online behaviors/visits on this computer.

Who Provided This Ad?

This ad has been brought to you by The extension

Why did I get this ad?

The extension is free because it is supported by placing one random ad at the bottom of the page on the various websites you go to. We do not place ads anywhere else, however, by placing that one random small ad at the bottom, you are helping The extension stay free and will continue to receive free updates with new and useful features.

When targeting an ad to you, The extension does not know your name or any other personal information about you. The extension recognizes a number stored in your browser on a 'cookie', and shows ads related to the interest and inferred demographic categories associated with that cookie. No other information is collected or stored with The extension.

How to Opt-Out

Enabled | Disable

Although your support would greatly help us to continue to bring you new and exciting features to The extension, we understand that you may not want to have the ad at the bottom of the page.

We thank you for using The extension and if you change your mind, you can always Opt-In to support us from time to time.

What is "Hide on this page" at the bottom of the ad mean?

Hide on this page enables you to hide the ad on a page and the ad will never return again unless you uninstall The extension. If you do uninstall and re-install the extension, you will need to go to specific websites to where you do not want advertising to be placed at the lower bottom of the page. You cannot re-enable an ad once you have selected the hide function.
