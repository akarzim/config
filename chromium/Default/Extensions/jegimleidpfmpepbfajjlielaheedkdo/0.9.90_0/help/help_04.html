How the colorPicker works

The colorPicker that appears in the far right bottom corner of the web-page works almost like in Adobe® Photoshop®.

Use your mouse to slide the cursors inside the color patches to choose the desired colors.
For accurate selection use the input fields and:

  • type in the desired number
  • use the arrow keys or the pageUp/pageDown keys to increase/decrease the values
  • click inside, hold and drag the mouse up and down to increase/decrease that single color value.

If the colorPicker is in your way you can resize it by dragging the right bottom corner to up to 4 different sizes or drag the app around the screen.

Save your colors in one of the 9 black squares by clicking on the 10th square with the circle in it. The saved colors will remain for every single domain so you can store your color sets for different web sites.

For more information about the colorPicker 0.9 and all its features and functions, see: dematte.at/colorPicker
