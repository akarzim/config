Color picking

Use your mouse to pick a color (range) on the whole area of the current web page.
Choose optional a square area between 1 x 1 and 101 x 101 pixels to calculate an average of all colors within this area that is displayed as the cursor.

The first pick after opening the app, dragging the colorPicker around or scrolling / resizing / reloading or changeing tabs in the browser, might be a little slower. This is just normal due to a massive calculation in the background but therefore you can rely on a very pixel exact and accurate result.

Use the 'Esc' key or click on the 'x' in the right top corner of the colorPicker to turn off the extension.
